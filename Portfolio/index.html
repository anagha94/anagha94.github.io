Seeking Internship Opportunities

"Good design is obvious, great design is transparent"

I could not agree more with this quote by Joe Sparano. Oftentimes, people adapt to technology rather than technology inconspicuously being part of their lives because of poor design that lacks empathy. I'm interested in making technology less intimidating and be an unobtrusive aid to people.

Having done my bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering, I am capable of keeping in mind both the potential and limitations of technology while designing, which will in turn ensure a good designer-developer rapport in the organisation. I have experience interacting with diverse individuals and can easily empathise with the people I have conversations with which helps me think beyond just the interface of the product while designing for potential users.

In my spare time I like to read, practice vocals, play my keyboard or dance. I am also passionate about photography, being with my camera and viewing the world through our eyes is my happy place.
